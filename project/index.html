About Us

We are the hub of a large community of American Civil War reenactors. Our mission is to connect people with American Civil War reenactment. Ranging from people who heard about it recently to seasoned reenactors who moved and want to find a new unit near them. We also can direct new people to places where they can get their reenactment gear from both sides.
